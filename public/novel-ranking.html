The Best Chinese and Korean Novels According to Zaky

My top 3 novels

Overgeared

It is a novel that has a great game system and fantastic character development

Desolate Era

It is my first chinese xianxia novel to be completed and my favorite til' this day. It has my favorite cultivation system in my opinion and has fantastic action.

I Shall Seal the Heaven

It is the funniest one among my favorite. Although the cultivation system is not my preference, it has great side characters, story, and comedy in it.
